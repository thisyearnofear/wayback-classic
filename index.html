About

Coupon DJ was created at Duke over a Saturday morning breakfast in 2007. Its servers ran on Amazon EC2, utilizing Fedora Core 7, Apache, mod_python, hand-coded XHTML & CSS.

This COPYPASTA VERSION pays homage to its creator, who with farcaster frames has set the stage for decentralisedcommerce seamlessly enabled via the social graph.

Its the early onchain era. (Not so) wayback it was the early internet era. Fun, experimental, for everyone.
